OUR STORY

Founded on Innovation

Founded in 2014, Aid was built with a vision to democratize high-quality healthcare. We realized that traditional medical systems were often slow and inaccessible, so we created a platform that bridges the gap between expert medical professionals and patients across the world.

Today, we are proud to serve over 1.5 million active clients, providing everything from routine checkups to complex neurological consultations through our integrated digital and physical network.
